Note: By accepting an interview, you agree to these terms.

Interview Terms

Anything you say in an interview can be used for any reason. (ex. an story for this webiste or instagram)

We will not include your name in any content on the website or our social medias (unless we tell you other wise)


If you would like us to end the interview and void all answers to your questions, we will do so as long as the interview has not conluded.


If you would like content removed after this time, please send a content removal request here.
