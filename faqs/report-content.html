How can I take down content that I don't belive should be on DHHS News?


To report/request content removal, please visit the delete account page. Select that you want content removed
and then tell us why you want it removed. We will contact you via the email on your account. You also email us at noreply.dhhsnews@gmail.com.


Why was my post/content/account removed?


We remove content for a number of reasons. Your content most likley violated our Terms of Service or contained content that we cannot host.
Your post may have been removed by another user via the content removal form. To appeal any moderation action, please
email us at noreply.dhhsnews@gmail.com. Please also check your email as we often explain to users
why content has been removed. If you did not use a valid email or phone number when you created your account, you cannot appeal or recive details
on content removal.
